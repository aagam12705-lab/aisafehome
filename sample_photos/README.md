# AI SafeHome Sample Photo Rules

Use only staged, non-patient photos.

Do not include:

- Faces
- Names
- Addresses
- Mail
- Bills
- Medication bottles
- Medical documents
- Real patient homes
- Real patient photos
- Medical records

## Recommended Staged Demo Photos

Create 10–15 staged photos before the final demo.

Suggested photos:

1. Safe living room
2. Living room with loose rug
3. Cord across walking path
4. Floor clutter with shoes or bags
5. Dim hallway
6. Bathroom without visible grab bars
7. Bathroom mat or slippery-looking floor
8. Stairs with possible handrail concern
9. Narrow hallway or blocked path
10. Kitchen with hard-to-reach items
11. Bedroom with clutter near bed
12. Safe room with no obvious hazards

## Photo Tips

- Use bright, clear photos.
- Avoid people in the photo.
- Avoid personal items.
- Use staged hazards only.
- Test both fake AI mode and real AI mode.
